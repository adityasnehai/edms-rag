# RFC-002: Operationalizing ADR-002

## problem_statement
The decision in ADR-002 introduces operational and reliability risks that must be addressed
before full rollout.

## proposed_solution
Implement ADR-002 behind feature flags.
Add dashboards, alerts, and gradual rollout plans.
Document rollback procedures.

## alternatives_considered
Do nothing – rejected due to scaling risks.
Full redesign – rejected due to timeline constraints.

## trade_offs
Improved scalability at the cost of higher operational complexity.
