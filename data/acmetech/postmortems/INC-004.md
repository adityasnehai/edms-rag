
# Incident Postmortem - INC-004

## Incident Summary
A production incident caused partial service degradation affecting
customer-facing APIs.

## Root Cause
A scaling limitation related to decisions captured in ADR-004.

## Contributing Factors
- Increased traffic
- Insufficient capacity planning

## Corrective Actions
- Implement improvements from RFC-004
- Enhance monitoring and alerting

## Lessons Learned
Early architectural decisions require continuous validation as systems scale.
