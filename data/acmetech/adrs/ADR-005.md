
# ADR-005: Core Platform Decision 5

## Status
Accepted (2022-06-15)

## Context
AcmeTech experienced scaling and reliability challenges in its core platform
as user traffic and data volume increased.

## Decision
Adopt solution 5 as part of the core platform architecture.

## Considered Options
1. Solution 5
2. Legacy approach
3. Fully managed third-party service

## Rationale
Solution 5 offered the best balance of scalability, operational control,
and alignment with existing AcmeTech engineering expertise.

## Consequences
Positive:
- Improved system scalability
- Reduced operational incidents

Negative:
- Increased system complexity
- Required additional team training
