# ADR-008: Kafka-Based Event Replay Mechanism

## context
Customers require the ability to replay historical workflow events after fixes.

## considered_options
1. Reprocess from PostgreSQL  
2. Kafka offset rewind  

## decision
Support controlled Kafka offset-based replay per tenant.

## rationale
Avoids custom re-ingestion pipelines.

## consequences
Uncontrolled replays can overload consumers (INC-008).
